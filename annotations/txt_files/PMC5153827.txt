TITLE: Erratum to: Methods and processes for development of a CONSORT extension for reporting pilot randomized controlled trials

  ABSTRACT:


BODY.ERRATUM:
After publication of the original article [1], it came to the authors' attention that there was an error in the Discussion section. In the final paragraph, "70,000 submissions" should have read "70,000 unique web accesses". The article has been updated to rectify this error.
